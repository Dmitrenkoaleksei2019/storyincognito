How to copy a list manually

If the profile is private or the auto-fetch fails:

  1. Open Instagram in a browser and go to your (or any public) profile.
  2. Tap "Following" to see the list of accounts.
  3. Select and copy the usernames (you can grab the whole block of text).
  4. Paste into the «Paste list» field above. With or without @ is fine — we clean it up.

Tip: the longer the list (50–400 accounts), the sharper the portrait.

FAQ

Is this actually accurate? ▼

No. It's a fun genre — the AI makes plausible guesses from the followings. It may sound uncannily right, but it's not a diagnosis. Treat it as entertainment.

Why can't you fetch by username? ▼

The account might be private, or Instagram throttled access to the followings list. Use the «Paste list» mode in that case.

How many accounts do I need? ▼

Minimum 10. Sweet spot is 50–400. Very short lists give blurry portraits.

Do you store my data? ▼

No. Neither the list nor the portrait is stored — only ephemeral server-side processing. See the privacy policy.

Are there limits? ▼

Yes — 3 portraits per hour, up to 30 per day, per IP. Helps keep the AI alive under bot traffic.
